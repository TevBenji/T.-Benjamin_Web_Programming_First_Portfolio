Web Pages and Their Purpose

  • Index Page (Home): Introduces the user to who I am and gives an overview of my career aspirations.
  • Professional Page: Contains my curriculum vitae (CV), vision, and mission statements.
  • Personality Page: Provides insight into my personality traits, hobbies, and hero's influence.
  • Personal Development Page: Reflects on my growth through books and learning experiences.
  • References Page: Lists all the references used for my website content.
  • Storyboard Page: Displays the wireframes and design plans for the entire website.

Theme and Design Elements

The website uses a clean and modern theme with a color scheme of dark blue (#081b29), light blue (#00abf0), and white (#ededed). This theme represents a professional and sleek look, targeting employers and peers in the tech industry.

Features of the Website

  • Navigation Bar: Allows users to move between different pages easily.
  • Clickable Image: A profile picture on the home page that opens the email client with a pre-filled message.
  • Social Media Links: External links to my Facebook, Twitter, and LinkedIn profiles.
  • Embedded Images: Wireframes and personality test results are embedded as images.
  • Email Links: "Hire Me" and "Let's Talk" buttons link to my email for professional inquiries.
